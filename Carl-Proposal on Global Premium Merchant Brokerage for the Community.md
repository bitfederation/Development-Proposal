Proposal on Global Premium Merchant Brokerage for the Community

In numerous countries and regions across the globe, many businesses such as entertainment venues and hotels operate round-the-clock. Since brokers are human beings who need proper rest, how should merchant settlements be handled for transactions occurring after 00:00 when brokers are off duty?
If merchants fail to receive fiat currency within 15 minutes, they will inevitably raise inquiries regarding delayed fund arrivals. In view of the above practical issues, I hereby put forward the following proposals:
All merchant orders placed after midnight shall be credited to accounts within 24 hours. Merchants will fully understand and acknowledge that funds will arrive in their accounts within this time frame.
The finance team can check real-time order processing status and track the whole progress of every transaction.
Establish a complete appeal mechanism, and allow the community system to independently complete order payment execution.
